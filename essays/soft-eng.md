## The Importance of Software Engineering

Software engineering is typically defined as "taking a systematic engineering approach to the maintenance and development of software." When I think of software engineering, I think of it as a necessary skill to have in order for a computer scientist to be successful in their field and not just a mandatory class that I inevitably had to take to get my B.S. in Computer Science. The reason why I think it is so important to have is because of its seemingly endless applications and diversity. Especially in a world dominated by technology and a time plagued by a pandemic, being a computer scientist with software engineering skills is now more urgent than ever. 

## Skills and Experiences I Hope to Develop
These reasons alone are enough for me to have an interest and motivation to study software engineering. However, growing up surrounded by technology and becoming savvy with it also has some influence over this interest as well as the realization that my future will eventually become even more dominated by technology. And so I thought that it would be to my benefit that I obtain a degree in Computer Science. I hope that by going down this career path and making reasonable professional decisions, I develop invaluable skills not only in software engineering or computer science but in practical settings such as leadership, project management, and collaboration. Personally, I feel that my skills in leadership are well developed due to various leadership positions I have had throughout high school and my time as a BoyScout. In my humble opinion, I would say that I have a natural affinity for leadership despite my introverted nature.

In addition to these skills, I also hope to gain invaluable experiences that will shape my future career and life. As previously mentioned, my personality tends to be that of a introvert but I am working hard to make connections and network with my peers and professors because I am aware of how important those relationships could be in the future. Additionally, I would like to acquire a summer internship to get my foot in the door as soon as possible as well as a mentor as I believe having someone you look up to that can give you honest advice and support is a huge factor in becoming successful. I am excited for the rest of my career as a computer scientist because I feel that there is still so much for me to learn and create.
